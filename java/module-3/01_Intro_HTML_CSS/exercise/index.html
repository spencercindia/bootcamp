Notes: Notes: Notes:
Ethiopian coffees tend to have a high acidity, syrupy mouthfeel and can have a winey quality to it. Kenyan coffees often have flavors of berries, lemongrass, bergamot, black tea and tomato. Guatemalan coffees are known for their mild chocolate/cocoa flavors in the aftertaste.
